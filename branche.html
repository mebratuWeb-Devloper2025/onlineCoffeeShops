About Amhara Region

The Amhara Region is the historical heartland of Ethiopia, home to ancient civilizations, UNESCO World Heritage sites, and the birthplace of Ethiopian coffee culture. Our branches are strategically located to serve both locals and visitors exploring this magnificent region.

Historical Sites

Near UNESCO World Heritage sites including Gondar Castles and Lalibela Rock Churches

Coffee Origin

Amhara region is known for producing some of Ethiopia's finest Arabica coffee beans

Cultural Hub

Center of traditional Ethiopian music, art, and cultural celebrations

Community Focus

Each branch supports local farmers and promotes sustainable coffee practices
