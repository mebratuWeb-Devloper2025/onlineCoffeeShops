ABOUT US

At Habesha Coffee Shop, we celebrate the rich flavors and traditions of Ethiopian coffee. Our mission is to provide a warm and welcoming space where coffee enthusiasts can experience authentic coffee culture, savor freshly brewed drinks, and enjoy meaningful conversations with friends and family. Every cup we serve is crafted with passion and care.
